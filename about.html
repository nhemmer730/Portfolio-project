Hi, I am Nicole Hemmer

Front-end developer, based in Massachusetts

I was a teacher of Students with Visual Impairments for 11 years. I was ready for a change when I found the SheCodes workshops. They were new challenge and introduction to coding. I started the Basics course and was amazed at how much I learned in those first three weeks. The information was organized and I loved that I could work along with the videos. At the end of the workshop, I had created my first ever landing page. I was hooked! After completing three more workshops, I have created many different pages, using HTML, Javascript, CSS as well as incorporating Bootstrap and APIs.
